Fried Ice Cream

Price: R17.99

Yield: 4

Fried ice cream is a dessert made of a scoop of ice cream that is frozen hard, breaded or coated in a batter and quickly deep-fried, creating a warm, crispy shell around the still-cold ice cream. An easy and fun dessert treat that will bring out the "kids" in all of us! Gluten-Free Vegan "Fried" Ice Cream! Balls of ice cream rolled in crushed gluten-free cookie crumbs! This is a must-have, no-bake recipe with your kids.
